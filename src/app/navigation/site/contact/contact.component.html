General Information

Venue :

The hotel is about 5 Km away from Hurghada International Airport. This 5 stars hotel has a unique location in the heart of Hurghada and offers “superb” services as rated by trip advisor.

Language :

All sessions and publications will be in English. There will be no translation. You will have no problem in communicating with people in Egypt, many can speak English and some speak French while the rest can still communicate somehow!

Visa :

It is always easy to get an online entry visa to Egypt. Few countries are exempted from getting an entry visa and others can get it upon arrival. However, the visa regulations change frequently. Please consult the Egyptian consulate in your country about visa requirements. You can also visit the Ministry of Foreign Affairs site at www.mfa.gov.eg then go to Consular Services section.

Travel :

Hurghada International airport is the second largest airport in Egypt. It receives direct flights from almost all European countries, Turkey, Jordan, Saudi Arabia and Kenya. There are some direct flights from Dubai also as well as so many others with a short stop in Cairo at very competitive prices. The other alternative is taking a flight to Cairo airport and then a connecting flight to Hurghada. Usually there are more than 5 flights daily from Cairo Airport to Hurghada which will ensure short waiting period for a connecting flight. Egypt Air has regular flights from many destinations around the world. Upon reaching Hurghada International Airport you need to take a taxi to the resort, it is about 15 minutes ride and costs about $ 8 – 10. Please negotiate the price at the beginning of the ride, remember that bargaining is fun in Egypt. Reaching Hurghada from Cairo by road is another alternative. The distance is about 500 kilometers. There are regular bus service that has 3-5 trips a day and takes about 6 hours. The organizing committee will be arranging a bus service on December 6th and another one on December 7th at 9 am from central location in Cairo to Hurghada and the fare will be announced later. Return trips will be organized on December 10th from Hurghada.

Currency :

The Egyptian pound (LE) is the official currency. One US$ is currently around LE 15.60. You can exchange money in any bank or money exchange. The exchange rate in hotels is more expensive. Please avoid personal transactions.

The organizing committee will issue a letter of invitation upon request and after receipt of registration fees.

Dress :

Usually casual but you would probably need a jacket for the opening ceremony, not for formality but because of the weather at night.

Weather :

The weather is usually pleasant in Hurghada during the first half of December. Average day temperature is 20 degrees centigrade and 17 degrees for the evening. Water temperature is said to be 24, so you might risk believe this and swim. It is usually sunny and dry and there is no need to bring your umbrella
