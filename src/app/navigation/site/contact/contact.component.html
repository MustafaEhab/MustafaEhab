Dress :

Usually casual but you would probably need a jacket for the opening ceremony, not for formality but because of the weather at night.

Weather :

The weather is usually pleasant in Hurghada during the first half of December. Average day temperature is 20 degrees centigrade and 17 degrees for the evening. Water temperature is said to be 24, so you might risk believe this and swim. It is usually sunny and dry and there is no need to bring your umbrella
