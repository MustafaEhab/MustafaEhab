While it will be possible to register on site, we do suggest that participants take advantage of the lower fee (early registration) and convenience of registering in advance. Those registered on site will obtain the certificates of attendance by email within 2 weeks after the conference.

Cancellation policy

All refunds will incur a 20% handling fee. No refunds will be made after October 31st, 2021. However, another delegate may be nominated as a replacement, up to two weeks before the conference. Cancellation must be in writing (by letter, fax or email) to the Congress Secretariat. All refunds will be made after the conference. Participants should state the bank details to which refunds should be made.
