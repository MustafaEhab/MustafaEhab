CALL FOR PAPERS

The Scientific Committee of the conference is inviting researchers, practitioners and academia from all over the world who are working or interested in the field of adolescent health, development and well-being to present their work during the conference. The committee encourages early career healthcare providers from the MENA region as well as Medical and Nursing students to submit their studies for possible presentation. Presentations could be in the form of oral presentations; workshops, debates or panel discussions. They could be finalized studies and research, reviews or programmatic reports. All submitted papers should be discussing relevant matters to the conference themes focusing on care of marginalized and disadvantaged adolescents as well as other issues related to equality. Suggestions for workshops, debates or panel discussions should be negotiated with the Chairperson of the Scientific Committee.

An abstract should be submitted for papers to be considered for oral presentation
